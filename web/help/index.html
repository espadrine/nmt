Game Help

Welcome to Not My Territory (Yet).

This is a game of collaborative exploration, construction and simulation. You are one of many leaders of a country, and your goal is to strive and overwhelm opposite countries. Once your empire has struck down all ennemies, your name will be put on the throne, and a new game will start.

Basics

At the start of the game, if you are the first one online, you will notice that you have a single person and a plot of farm land. You can move that person by left-clicking on her tile, and left-clicking on the tile you want her to go to. You can make that person build, destroy the building she's on, and reproduce by right-clicking on the tile.
