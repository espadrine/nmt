Game Help

Welcome to Not My Territory (Yet).

This is a game of collaborative exploration, construction and simulation. You are one of many leaders of a country, and your goal is to strive and overwhelm opposite countries. Once your empire has struck down all ennemies, your name will be put on the throne, and a new game will start.

Basics

At the start of the game, if you are the first one online, you will notice that you have three starving folks dancing around on a tile. You can move them by left-clicking on their tile, pressing the "travel" button, and left-clicking on the tile you want to go to. You can make those folks build, destroy the building they're on, and split groups by clicking on the corresponding buttons at the top left.

Advanced controls

There are several mapped keys to allow you to perform actions faster.

Travel T
Construct C
Fork (split) group F
Destroy building ` (backtick)
Navigate the map ←→↑↓ WASD
Construct building 0-9
Zoom in / out - = +
