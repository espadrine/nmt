War

When a group of folks goes on a tile with enemies, both your group and the enemy's group lose the same number of folks. If you have more people, the enemy's group is destroyed, and whatever remains of your group occupies the tile. Otherwise, your group dies.

Having weapons doubles your group's firepower. For instance, a group of 2 with weapons going against an unarmed group of 3 will win.

Being located in a vegetation area (such as swamps, meadow, forests, or taiga) protects you from harm: you get a 1.5x firepower bonus.

You can also make your enemy surrender by having an enemy tile surrounded by at least 2 tiles around them while you attack them. They will instantly switch camp, increasing your population (but not your population cap), and you won't lose any folks.

Given the loss that you must suffer to really make your enemy suffer, coordinated war is advised.
