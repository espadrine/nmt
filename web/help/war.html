War

When a group of folks goes on a tile with enemies, both your group and the enemy's group lose the same number of folks. If you have more people, the enemy's group is destroyed, and whatever remains of your group occupies the tile. Otherwise, your group dies.

Having weapons doubles your group's firepower. For instance, a group of 2 with weapons going against an unarmed group of 3 will win, as if they were 4.

Fighting is tiresome: any group that fights will lose 5 units of food.

Being located in a vegetation area (such as swamps, meadow, forests, or taiga) protects you from harm: you get a 1.5x firepower bonus. On the other hand, attacking from high ground (attacking steppes from hills, for instance) gives you a 1.5x firepower bonus.

Each vehicle has its own pros and cons as well (bonuses give a 1.5x firepower increase, maluses give a 0.5x firepower decrease):

Vehicle Bonus against Malus against Terrain bonus
Car Artillery Plane Steppe, Meadow
Boat Car, Artillery Plane Water, Swamp
Artillery Car Boat Hill, Forest
Plane Car, Artillery Boat Mountain, Taiga

You can also make your enemy surrender by surroundering an enemy tile before winning the battle against it. If it is surrounded by n tiles, n/6th of them will instantly switch camp, increasing your population (but not your population cap).

Given the loss that you must suffer to really make your enemy suffer, planning and coordination is advised.
