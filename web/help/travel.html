Travel and Speed

How fast traveling through a tile is depends on its type. Traveling through mountains and going through slopes takes more time. Some tiles have special conditions to be able to cross them, such as water, swamp, and walls.

Zone Distance
Road 1
Water 2
Swamp 3
Steppe 2
Meadow 3
Hills 4
Forest 8
Mountain 16
Taiga 24
Wall 32

Each person has a certain speed, depending on how she is equiped. By default, her speed is 8, that is, she can walk a cumulated distance of 8 at once. With a car, her speed would be 16. On a boat, it would be 20. By plane, 32. However, if a tile contains some ennemies, the person cannot go past that tile. She has to stop on it and fight.

Water and swamps cannot be crossed barefoot, nor with a car. You need boats to sail through them, although planes also work.
