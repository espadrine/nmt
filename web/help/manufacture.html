Manufacture

A number of buildings can provide valuable artifacts to groups of people. In every case, a group must walk on the manufacturer's tile to get the objects they need.

  • A Factory provides cars. They increase ground speed to 16.
  • The Docks provide boats. They sail through water and swamps at a speed of 20. Building docks requires two tiles.
  • The Airport provides planes. They fly at a speed of 32. Building an airport requires four tiles.
  • The Gunsmith provides weapons. They double a group's ability to attack.
