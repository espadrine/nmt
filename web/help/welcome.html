Game Help

Welcome to Not My Territory (Yet).

Basics

At the start of the game, if you are the first one online, you will notice that you have three starving folks dancing around on a tile. You can move them by left-clicking on their tile, pressing the second button, and left-clicking on the tile you want to go to. You can make those folks build through the third button. Finally, you can locate enemies and important locations through the fourth button.

You start in your camp's spawn area. The first thing I recommend you do is zoom out (press the "-" key) and find people in opposite camps through the "places" (fourth) button. Then, focus on your camp and improve it. If you're starting a game, your folks are starving: make them build a farm, then a residence. That will help you increase your population.

Winning

You can win a game by:
  • Intellectual Victory: build two space missions and a monument.
    Best if you have an abundance of fuel.
  • Industrial Victory: build two stock exchanges and a space mission.
    Best if you have an abundance of metal.
  • Prosperity Victory: build two monuments and a stock exchange.
    Best if you have an abundance of wealth.
  • Conquest Victory: kill two leaders. (Leaders are not implemented yet.)
