Game Help

Welcome to Not My Territory (Yet).

This is a game of collaborative exploration, construction and simulation. You are one of many leaders of a country, and your goal is to strive and overwhelm opposite countries. Once an enemy has been struck down, if your empire is the greatest, your name will be put on the throne, and a new game will start.

Basics

At the start of the game, if you are the first one online, you will notice that you have three starving folks dancing around on a tile. You can move them by left-clicking on their tile, pressing the "travel" button, and left-clicking on the tile you want to go to. You can make those folks build and split groups by clicking on the corresponding buttons at the top left. Finally, you can locate enemies and treasures through the "places" button.

You start in your camp's spawn area. The first thing I recommend you do is zoom out (press the "-" key) and find people in opposite camps through the "places" (fourth) button. Then, focus on your camp and improve it. If you're starting a game, your folks are starving: make them build a farm, then a residence. That will help you increase your population.

Winning

You can win a game by:
  • Supremacy Victory: be the most populous country when one country is eradicated.
  • Industrial Victory: collect 13 metal.
  • Intellectual Victory: acquire 3 universities hold by enemies.
