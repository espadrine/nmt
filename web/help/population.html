Population

Each camp's population is the number of folks that are part of that camp on the whole map. That population is always higher than or equal to the population cap. It can be higher thanks to surrendering enemies, and will usually decrease because of wars,

The population cap is directly equal to the number of homes (2 homes in a residence, and 6 in a skyscraper) that you occupy. Your population increases up to the population cap instantly. Every new folk that you get is added on one of your home tiles. As a result, building more houses and invading cities both help you get more people.

However, you can only grow up to the population limit, set to 100 folks (= 1 Health point). Past that, you can increase that limit by 100 folks for every university that you control.

If you go past the Health limit without universities to back it up, the social injustices will cause the people to revolt. Then, a piece of your nation will secede.
